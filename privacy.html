Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

Jared's Demo Line ("we," "us") provides missed-call text-back services for local businesses. This policy describes how we handle information for our SMS messaging service.

Information We Collect

When you call or text our business number, we collect: your phone number, the content of your messages, and any details you choose to share (such as your name, the service you need, and preferred callback time).

How We Use It

We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry, arrange a callback, and provide the service you requested. Message content may be processed by automated software to generate responses.

SMS Consent & Third Parties

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, excluding aggregators and providers of the text message services necessary to deliver messages.

Opting Out

Reply STOP to any message to stop receiving texts. Reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your conversation.

Data Retention

Conversation data is retained only as long as needed to complete your service request and for reasonable business records, then deleted.
