Jared's Demo Line — SMS Program & Opt-In

Effective date: July 5, 2026

Jared's Demo Line provides a missed-call text-back service for local service businesses (plumbers, electricians, and other trades). When a customer calls a participating business and the call isn't answered, our system replies by text message to capture the caller's service request and preferred callback time, so the business can follow up. This page explains how customers consent to receive those text messages.

Our messaging number

(361) 314-6692

This number is published on the participating business's listing, voicemail greeting, and marketing so that customers know how to reach the business by phone or text.

How customers opt in

  1. A customer places a phone call to our messaging number (361) 314-6692 because they want service from the business.
  2. If the call is not answered, our system sends the customer a text message inviting them to reply with their service request. This message discloses the program, message frequency, rates, and how to get help or stop.
  3. By replying to that text, the customer gives express consent to receive conversational, customer-care text messages related to their own inquiry.

Consent is not a condition of any purchase. We do not send marketing or promotional messages. Customers only receive texts in direct response to a call they placed to the business.

What the first text looks like

Below is the exact consent message a customer receives after an unanswered call. It carries all required disclosures:

Incoming call to (361) 314-6692 — not answered
